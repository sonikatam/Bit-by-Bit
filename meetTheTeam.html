The Bit-By-Bit executive board is a team of aspiring computer science students who are passionate about bridging the digital divide and empowering fellow students and communities through technology. With combined experience as tutors, software engineering interns, and community leaders, the Bit-By-Bit executive board is uniquely qualified to guide the organization towards its mission of providing young students with skills and resources needed to succeed in the technological world. The board is dedicated to continuing this work, and to developing innovative new programs and initiatives that will help even more people to unlock their full potential.
