Sponsoring

What does it mean to sponsor?

Sponsoring is way for your organization to support Bit-by-Bit’s mission of increasing youth involvement in computer science within our community. Through your investment, you can also benefit with increased exposure, branding, and networking opportunities with future technology leaders!

What does it mean to donate?

Donating is a way for anyone to support Bit-by-Bit’s goals. Your donation would enable us to introduce new technology and activities for our members to further prepare them for the industry. In addition, donations would aid in expanding to other communities to impact more youth!
