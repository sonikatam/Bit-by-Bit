About Us

Bit-by-Bit is dedicated to fostering involvement in tehcnology and coding for students in grades 6-12. At the middle school level, students are provided with instruction from our team of mentors to learn about the basics of programming. At the high school level, students are provided with unique opportunities to learn from professionals in technology and build upon their skills. Every year, students have the chance to present the skills that they have accumulated at our annual conference. We believe that through our club, students will have exposure to technology related activities and build a found for continued learning beyond their K-12 years.
